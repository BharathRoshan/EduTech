Management

Event management is the application of project management to the creation and development of small and/or large-scale personal or corporate events such as festivals, conferences, ceremonies, weddings, formal parties, concerts, or conventions.

Marketing

Event marketing is a strategy marketers use to promote their brand, product, or service with an in-person or real-time engagement.

Uniqueness

Unique Events are interactions with content by a single user within a single session that can be tracked separately from pageviews or screenviews.

Design

Event designers have an eye for creatively using space. They are often employed by catering companies, wedding designers, event entertainment companies, and firms that handle corporate events.

Creativity

The act of turning new and imaginative ideas into reality. Creativity involves thinking, then producing.
